About the Smurfs

The Smurfs are a fictional group of small, blue creatures who live in mushroom-shaped houses in the forest. They were created by Belgian cartoonist Peyo in 1958 and have since become popular worldwide. The Smurfs are known for their happy-go-lucky attitude and their signature phrase "smurfy".
